About Ambrosai Wellness

Wellness that supports real, everyday life

Ambrosai Wellness is a women-focused health consulting brand based in Dubai, UAE. The core mission is to guide clients toward lasting wellness through balanced food, practical routines, and healthy lifestyle habits.

Rather than extreme restrictions, the business emphasizes:

  • Consistency over perfection
  • Familiar, realistic food patterns
  • Sustainable fat-loss and wellness outcomes
  • Culturally aware coaching for Indian women and families
